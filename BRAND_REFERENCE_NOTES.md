# Alicyn Instagram brand reference notes

Source reviewed: https://www.instagram.com/alicyn.mx/

Access date: 2026-05-18

## What was accessible

The public Instagram profile data was accessible without login through Instagram's web profile response. I reviewed the bio, recent post captions, pinned/product thumbnails and carousel thumbnails. Temporary Instagram media URLs were used only for reference; no Instagram images were embedded into the theme.

## Brand tone

- Direct, helpful and conversational.
- Speaks to people with piercings and to piercers/studios.
- Uses confidence-building language around questions, DM support and practical care.
- Repeated ideas include piercing aftercare, cuidar perforaciones, pedidos por DM/página, envíos and apoyo para estudios.

## Repeated language and themes

- “Cuidado post-piercing por Alicyn MX”
- “Cuida tus perforaciones”
- Moments when piercings need more attention: sweat, bath, bump, discomfort, jewelry change and accidental contact.
- “Cualquier duda” / message-based support.
- Mexico shipping, with CDMX and Edo. Mex highlighted in the bio.
- Piercer/studio distribution, starter packs, wholesale and point-of-sale education.

## Visual style

- Clean educational carousel format.
- Bold, high-contrast headline typography.
- Soft aqua, teal, lavender and white/gray backgrounds.
- Product bottle shown upright on light aqua or clean editorial backgrounds.
- Droplet-style brand mark and lowercase `alicyn` logo treatment.
- Bubbles/liquid references appear in older graphics; the Shopify theme translates this as subtle aqua/lavender bands rather than heavy decorative effects.

## Product positioning used in the theme

The theme keeps Alicyn as targeted piercing aftercare for key moments of sensitivity, exposure or discomfort. It borrows the Instagram idea of being there when perforations need extra attention, while staying safer and more precise:

- No automatic daily-use positioning.
- No medical promises.
- No claims about changing the natural timing of the process.
- Clear professional-consultation guidance for warning signs.
- 100 ml positioning remains current for this Shopify product, even though older Instagram posts show older presentation language.

## Landing page updates made from this reference

- Hero now uses Alicyn's real aftercare vocabulary and community language.
- Benefit cards now map to Instagram-style scenarios: sweat, street, pressure, jewelry changes, bumps and studio use.
- “Cuándo sí usar Alicyn” now feels closer to the educational carousel format.
- Studio section now speaks directly to piercers and point-of-sale support.
- FAQ and chat quick questions now include bump, shipping, piercer/studio and WhatsApp support language.
- Visual accents now include aqua/lavender tones and a droplet-style text logo while preserving the premium dark theme.
